Microsoft Edge is my default browser on Mac, as of date, and for good reason. It’s lightning fast, incredibly secure, and seamlessly integrates with AI tools like Copilot for smarter browsing. The built-in vertical tabs, collections, and immersive reader make multitasking and content consumption a breeze. I love the efficiency of sleeping tabs and performance optimization, which keep my Mac running smoothly. Edge’s deep integration with Microsoft 365 is a huge plus for productivity, and the customization options are top-notch. With robust privacy settings and tracking prevention, it’s the perfect blend of speed, intelligence, and security—hands down my go-to browser every day.

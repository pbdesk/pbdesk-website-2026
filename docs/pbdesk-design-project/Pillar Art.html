Bits, Bites & Blog

A consistent visual system for the three content pillars on pbdesk.com — built on the 2026 design tokens (Onest type, zinc neutrals, brand blue) and tuned to read on both light and dark surfaces. Each pillar gets a banner, a square share card, and a circular avatar.

The concept

Bits

The dev side. AI, code, tools, productivity. A rounded code-bracket tile in brand blue with a sky/amber spark — terminals, repos, things that compile.

Bites

The wellness side. Fitness, nutrition, mindfulness. A leaf tile in emerald with a heart-pulse accent — small, regular, healthy bites.

Blog

The bridge. Long-form reflections that connect tech & wellness. An open book tile in violet/blue, with a dotted bridge tying the code & leaf endpoints together.
